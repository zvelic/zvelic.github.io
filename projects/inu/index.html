Inu-Health

Inu Health is a digital Cognitive behavioural therapy (CBT) program for managing menstrual related symptoms for women who wish to take back control of their lives again.

Target Group:

Women who constantly suffer from menstrual pains, women who are under higher risks of gynecological disease.

My Inputs:

I created and designed the Inu Health logo as well as a detailed design guideline introducing the use of the brand colors and typography. I collaborated closely with the two main founders of Inu Health and designed the company’s landing page including illustrated elements, the high fidelity design of the most functional screens of the product, and a short video of the brand’s vision.
